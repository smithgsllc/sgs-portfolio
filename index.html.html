YOUR PUBLIC SERVICE PARTNER

Our Story

Smith Government Services, LLC, founded in 2023, is a highly skilled professional firm based in Virginia. Led by Tiara D. Smith, PMP, with a focus on government contracts, our expertise and dedication to excellence set us apart. At the core of our mission is the commitment to providing top-notch services to governmental organizations at every level.

Mission

A federal-focused Program and Project Management consulting practice providing mission-aligned support across government and defense environments. Services emphasize governance, execution oversight, IT program delivery, and organizational performance improvement — built on 7+ years of direct experience within DoD, DISA, and federal law enforcement environments.
